How CoinQuest works

Four steps from sidewalk to spendable.

  1. 01

    Put on your glasses

    Works with Meta Quest 3, Quest Pro, and Ray-Ban Meta (limited features). Open the CoinQuest app and your surroundings light up with collectible coins.

  2. 02

    Walk your usual routes

    Coins spawn at GPS-anchored points along pedestrian-friendly paths. Your commute, your dog walk, your coffee run — they all become collection routes.

  3. 03

    Look + tap to collect

    Glance at a coin and tap to grab it. Chain coins quickly for combo multipliers. Daily challenges drop bonus trails across town.

  4. 04

    Redeem in the wallet

    Cash out to PayPal or Venmo, swap for Amazon or Starbucks gift cards, donate to community causes, or save for limited-edition cosmetics.

Common questions

Is this gambling?

No. CoinQuest is a skill- and activity-based rewards game. You don't pay to play and you don't wager coins. Coins are earned through walking and challenges, not chance-based purchases.

What does it cost?

The app itself is free. Optional cosmetics can be purchased, but every cash and gift-card payout is funded by us, not by other users.

Will I owe taxes on payouts?

Possibly. In the U.S., redemptions over $600 in a calendar year trigger a 1099 form, which we'll mail you. Your accountant is the right person to ask about your specific situation.

What about my privacy and location data?

Location is processed on-device whenever possible. We store the minimum needed to validate coin collection and prevent spoofing. You can request a full export or deletion anytime.

Is it safe to walk while wearing AR glasses?

We only spawn coins along sidewalks and pedestrian zones, never in roadways. The app freezes coins at unsafe crossings and shows obstacle and traffic alerts in your view. Always look where you're going — coins will wait.

What ages can play?

CoinQuest is open to users 13 and up. Players under 18 need a guardian to approve cash redemptions, and we don't process payouts to minors directly.

The legal stuff, in plain English

We take this seriously. Here's the short version of what governs a cashless rewards community.

Sweepstakes & gaming law

CoinQuest is structured as a no-purchase-necessary skill and activity rewards program. Coins are earned by walking, not by paying or wagering, so it sits outside U.S. gambling definitions in every state we've reviewed.

Money transmission

Cash payouts are processed through licensed partners (PayPal, Venmo, and our banking-as-a-service provider). CoinQuest never holds custody of fiat funds on your behalf.

Tax reporting

U.S. users who redeem more than $600 in cash equivalents per calendar year receive a 1099-MISC. Non-U.S. users receive jurisdiction-appropriate documentation.

Privacy & location

We follow GDPR and CCPA standards globally. Location data used for coin validation is processed on-device when possible, encrypted in transit, and never sold.

Minors & COPPA

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Users 13–17 use a restricted account with parental approval required for cash redemptions.

AR safety

The app is designed to keep your eyes on the world, not the overlay. Coins never spawn in roadways and pause during unsafe movement. Use of CoinQuest is at your own risk — please walk responsibly.
